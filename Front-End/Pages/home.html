FaQs

What is Promotex? ▼
Promotex connects customers with local brands for shopping and renting clothing, beauty, and food products.
How can my brand or shop join Promotex? ▼
Joining Promotex is easy! Simply register for a seller account through our "Become a Seller" page. After submitting your brand details and product information, our team will review your application. Once approved, you can start listing your products and reaching a wider community of customers who value local brands.
Can I return or exchange products purchased through Promotex? ▼
Yes, Promotex allows returns and exchanges according to each seller’s return policy. You can view the specific return conditions on the product page before purchasing. If you need assistance, our support team is always available to guide you through the process and ensure a smooth experience.
How can I buy products from local brands on Promotex? ▼
You can browse a wide selection of products from verified local brands directly on Promotex. When you find a product you like, simply add it to your cart and proceed to secure checkout. After placing your order, you will receive confirmation and tracking details to stay updated on your delivery.
